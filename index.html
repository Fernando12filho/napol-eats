Welcome to NapolEats!

Family-owned and passionately crafted, experience the authentic taste of our Neapolitan pizza.

How We Began

We are a family-owned pizzeria dedicated to bringing you the best in pizza. Our journey started with a passion for Neapolitan pizza and a commitment to quality. We now bring our delicious pizzas to your events with our mobile food truck and offer vacuum-packed, pre-made pizzas for you to enjoy at home. Every bite carries the taste of tradition, thanks to our special oven that mimics the flavor of a wood-fired stove.

How Our Events Work

Hosting an event? Our food truck is the perfect addition to any gathering, offering fresh, hot, and delicious pizzas on-site. We handle all the cooking, so you and your guests can enjoy a seamless and delightful experience. For those who prefer to enjoy our pizzas at home, we offer pre-made, vacuum-sealed pizzas that are easy to prepare and packed with flavor.
